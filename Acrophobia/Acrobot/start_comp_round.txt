start_comp_round

Example use: start_comp_round 1 60000 1 "ACRO" "category"

Used to start a composition round.

1st Detail - Unknown.
2nd Detail - The length of the round timer in milliseconds.
3rd Detail - The round number.
4th Detail - The acronym. (Must be in all caps)
5th Detail - The category of the round. (Must be a specific category for the background images to appear)
