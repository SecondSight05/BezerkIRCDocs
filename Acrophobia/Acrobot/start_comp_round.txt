start_comp_round

Example use: start_comp_round 1 1 1 "ACRO" "category"

1st Detail - Unknown
2nd Detail - Unknown (May have to do with the timer. If this is set to anything other than 0, the timer kind of works?)
3rd Detail - Unknown
4th Detail - The acronym. (Must be in all caps)
5th Detail - The category of the round.
