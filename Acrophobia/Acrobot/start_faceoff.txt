start_faceoff

Example use: start_faceoff 0 20000 0 "ip0123456789" "ip1234567890"

Used after a voting round has finished, once a player has reached 30 or more points.

1st Detail - Unknown.
2nd Detail - The length of the round timer in milliseconds.
3rd Detail - If there was a point tie for the 2nd place spot. (The person who was overall faster in the previous composition round gets the spot)
4th Detail - The IRC username of the player who reached 30 or more points.
5th Detail - The IRC username of the 2nd place player.
