start_faceoff_comp_round

Example use: start_faceoff_comp_round 1 20000 1 "ACRO"

Used to start a composition round for the two faceoff players.

1st Detail - Unknown.
2nd Detail - The length of the round timer in milliseconds.
3rd Detail - Round music. (See below)
4th Detail - The acronym. (Must be in all caps)

The 3rd Detail changes what the faceoff round's music will be.
1 = Round 1 Music
2 = Round 2 Music
3 = Round 3 Music
4 = Round 2 Music
5 = Round 3 Music
6 = Final Scores Music
