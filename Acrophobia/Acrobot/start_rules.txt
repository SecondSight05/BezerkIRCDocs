start_rules

Example use 1: start_rules faceoff_voter 20000
Example use 2: start_rules faceoff_player 20000

Used to show the rules for the faceoff rounds.

1st Detail - "faceoff_player" is used if you are one of the top two players, and "faceoff_voter" is used for anyone else.
2nd Detail - The length of the round timer in milliseconds.
