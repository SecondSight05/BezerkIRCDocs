start_categories

Example use: start_categories 0 0 0 "ip1234567890"

Used once a voting round has finished. The player who got the most points in the previous voting round chooses the category.

1st Detail - Unknown.
2nd Detail - Unknown.
3rd Detail - Unknown.
4th Detail - The IRC username of the player that chooses the category.
